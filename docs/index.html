Introduction

Econometric ideas are only as useful as their implementations. The course pairs economic intuition with real data practice, making sure you can go from raw data to defensible results.

What you will practice

  • Collecting and validating real-world economic datasets.
  • Cleaning, joining, and reshaping data for analysis.
  • Building reproducible notebooks and scripts.
  • Communicating insights with clear visuals.

What you will build

  • Reusable data pipelines for empirical research.
  • Simulation tools for econometric intuition.
  • Models for prediction and policy evaluation.
  • Workflow habits that scale to research projects.
